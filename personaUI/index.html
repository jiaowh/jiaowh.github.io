“Every brand has a Palace. I find the treasure.”

Kaede Arlow is an art director and visual designer working at the seam where editorial systems meet game-grade motion. She builds identities that don't sit still — type that lunges, grids that refuse to behave, and interfaces that feel like the moment before a battle starts.

Previously lead designer for record labels, fighting games and three coffee brands that all somehow wanted to look like a riot. Now running a one-woman atelier and taking hearts on commission.
