Craft

Shadowbox art — each piece is cut, moulded and layered by hand so that flat paper becomes a scene with real depth. Characters and creatures from games, anime and manga brought into three dimensions, one layer at a time.

The Process

01

Design

Break the image into depth layers — foreground, midground, background.

02

Cut

Precisely cut each layer from printed paper or card stock.

03

Mould

Shape and curl the paper pieces to give each layer dimension.

04

Assemble

Stack and glue the layers with spacers to create real 3D depth.
